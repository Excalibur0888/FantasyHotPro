Frequently Asked Questions

To create a fantasy cricket team, sign up on Fantasy Hot, select the cricket match you want to participate in, and choose 11 players from the real-life squads. Make sure to balance your team with batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders, and a wicketkeeper. Assign a captain and vice-captain to earn extra points!

Points are awarded based on player performance in real-life matches. Batsmen earn points for runs, bowlers for wickets, and fielders for catches and run-outs. The captain earns 2x points, and the vice-captain earns 1.5x points. Check the "Rules" section for a detailed breakdown.

Fantasy Hot offers public leagues, private leagues, head-to-head contests, and grand leagues. Public leagues are open to all, while private leagues allow you to compete with friends. Grand leagues have higher stakes and bigger rewards.

To join a private league, create or enter a unique league code provided by your friend. Go to the "Leagues" section, select "Private League," and enter the code. You can then compete with your friends in a customized league.

If a player in your team gets injured, you cannot replace them once the match starts. However, you can make changes to your team before the match begins. Always check player updates and news before finalizing your team.

To withdraw your winnings, go to the "Wallet" section in your account. Follow the instructions to link your payment method and request a withdrawal. Withdrawals are processed within 3-5 business days, depending on your payment provider.
