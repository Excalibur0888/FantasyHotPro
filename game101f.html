101f Rules

1. Building Your Team

Create a balanced squad of skilled players to dominate the court:

  • Setters: Precision experts orchestrating plays and assists.
  • Attackers: Offensive specialists delivering powerful hits.
  • Defenders: Masters of digs, blocks, and saves.
  • Versatile Players: Athletes capable of adapting to multiple roles.

2. Scoring Breakdown

Earn points based on individual and team performance:

  • Offense: 5 points for an ace, 3 points for a successful attack.
  • Defense: 4 points for a block, 2 points for a dig.
  • Teamwork: 3 points per assist, 8 points for a double-double.
  • Penalties: -2 points for a service error, -3 points for fouls.
