Understanding the Game Format

Laser247 offers a variety of formats to suit your playstyle:

Head-to-Head: Compete directly against another manager. Earn points based on stats like kills, blocks, and digs. The highest score wins the round.

Points League: Accumulate points based on player performances. Actions like aces, assists, and saves contribute to your total.

Roto League: Teams are ranked in each statistical category. The combined ranking determines the overall winner.

Whether you’re in for the long haul or prefer quick challenges, Laser247 has a format for everyone.

Laser247 Rules

1. Building Your Team

Assemble a team of players with diverse skills, ensuring balance across positions:

  • Setters: Masters of playmaking and assists.
  • Spikers: Offensive powerhouses delivering points.
  • Defenders: Experts in digs and saves.
  • All-Rounders: Players who excel in multiple roles.

2. Scoring Breakdown

Points are awarded based on performance:

  • Offense: 5 points for an ace, 3 points for a successful spike.
  • Defense: 2 points for a dig, 4 points for a block.
  • Teamwork: 3 points per assist, 10 points for a triple-double.
  • Penalties: -2 points for service errors, -3 points for fouls.
